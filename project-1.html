Project

An endless legend

Project Overview

This group project was inspired by the Arab folkloric character Umm Al-Subyan. My individual contribution focused on designing the opening and closing frames, where I highlighted the authentic Egyptian environment through detailed houses and streets.

In the final frame, I worked on presenting the story's twist in a visually impactful way. This project reflects my creative approach to visual storytelling, environment design, and building. suspense through artistic details.
