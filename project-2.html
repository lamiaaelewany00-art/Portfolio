Project

Ana Wa Anta Wa Baba Fi El-Meshmish

Project Overview

Inspired by the iconic Egyptian TV series directed by Mohamed Fadel and written by Osama Anwar Okasha, this project reimagines the show's themes of 2D animated corruption and social values through a opening sequence.

LI recreated backgrounds and characters based on the original scenes, while adding my own cartoon aesthetic to give the work a fresh artistic identity. The project highlights my ability to blend cultural references with creative storytelling and translate real-world narratives into engaging.
